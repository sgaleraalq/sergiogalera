About Me

Hi! I'm Sergio, a Bioinformatician and Android Developer based in Pamplona, Spain.

I started my professional career by studying a Bachelor's Degree in Biochemistry in the University of Navarra.

After graduating, I moved to Denmark and decided to continue my studies by doing a Master's Degree in Bioinformatics at Aarhus University.

In Denmark is where I discovered my passion for programming and software development. I started programming with Python and and after finishing my Master's Degree, I moved back to Spain and started working as a Bioinformatician while learning how to develop Android applications in my free time.

I consider myself a self-taught person, always eager to learn new things and improve my skills. I am problem-solver and goal-oriented person who loves to work in a team and share knowledge with others.
